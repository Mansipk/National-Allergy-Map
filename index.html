National Allergy Map



Allergic diseases are among common chronic disorders affecting lives of more than one billion people worldwide. Approximately, 20 to 30% of Indian population suffers from allergic rhinitis and 15% develop asthma. Currently, more than 25% of the total Indian population is sensitized with different forms of allergens such as pollens from trees, grass and weed, fungal spores, house dust mites, insect debris, animal epithelia, food.

The National Allergy Map database has been curated with the aim of providing a holistic overview of allergens prevalent in Indian population.

Bioinformatics Center- Sri Venkateswara College, University of Delhi
